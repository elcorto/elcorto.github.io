pwtools.mttk.barostat_mass_w¶

pwtools.mttk.barostat_mass_w(freq, temp, nf=None, ndim=3)[source]¶

Fictitious mass W for the barostat itself for isotropic cell fluctuations.

Abinit: bmass for ionmov 13 + optcell 1.

Parameters:

freq : scalar

Typical phonon frequency [Hz].

temp : scalar

Temperature [K].

nf : scalar

Number of degrees of freedom. Usually ndim*natoms for no constranis with ndim==3 (x,y,z).

ndim : scalar

Number of dimensions. Usually ndim==3 (x,y,z).
Returns:

W : barostat mass
